Internet Humor

There are a lot of funny and cool things that are on the internet. These are some of the ones that I find the most amusing.

Boil The Frog

Boil The Frog is a website that will seamlessly create a playlist that transitions you from one artist to another regardless of genre. I think this is pretty amusing considering that you can put some wild genre changes in it and still it will create a playlist that transition.

Boil The Frog

Radioooo

Radiooo is a website that allows you to listen to the radio at any point in the world at any time going back to 1900.

This is amusing becuase I cannot believe the type of music that people of certain time periods and eras were listening to

Radioooo

Pointer Painter

Pointer Painter is a website that finds where your pointer is located on the screen and generates a picture with somebody pointing at your on-screen pointer. This is a really amusing website as there are some ridiculous pictures that end up generating from it. I highly reccomend visiting this website if you want to laugh and burn a couple minutes from your day
