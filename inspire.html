What makes a hero?

Heroes are somebody who we can aspire to be and to model ourselves after. Somebody we take morale and ethical reflections from.

My personal hero is Phil Esposito who overcame a lot of adversity and somebody I could look up to and aspire to be. I modeled my game after this player as I initially had a lot of the same qualities as him and would later find how best to be like him.
